IRCTC Authorised Partner - Train Booking


Book IRCTC or Indian Railway train tickets online quickly and easily on ixigo’s train mobile app or website using your IRCTC user ID and login. You will be paying ₹0 payment gateway fee on train booking and reservation via UPI. We, being IRCTC authorised partner, offer Tatkal ticket booking service online on our mobile app and website. You can also book special trains online on our mobile app.

Once you have booked your ticket online, check your PNR status and know the chances of your ticket confirmation. Confirm your train's live running status (or use ixigo’s where is my train feature).

Online Train Ticket Booking

  • Open the ixigo website or download mobile app. Click on book train tickets section.
  • Select the source and destination Indian Railway stations from the list.
  • Pick a date of departure and travel class-AC First Class, Sleeper Class, Second AC, Third AC, AC Chair car.
  • Log in using your IRCTC user ID and password. Create a new IRCTC username if you don't have one.
  • Complete the payment and reservation process using your IRCTC login details.
  • You will then receive a confirmation on your registered email id and SMS on mobile number.
